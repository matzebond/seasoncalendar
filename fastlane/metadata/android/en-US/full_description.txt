<p>This App shows you which foods are in Season right now and are thus available from nearby produce. Not only are they cheaper and tastier, but also their transport is less harmful to our environment.</p>
<p>Currently the data is only available for the Central Europe region. More
regions and languages are coming soon.</p>
<p>
<b>Features:</b>
<br>* Boasts the biggest collection of foods
<br>* Lets you pick and view your favorites
<br>* Open Source
<br>* Can be run on a variety of android devices
<br>* Data presets currently available: Central Europe
</p>
<p><i>Please keep in mind that the actual availability of foods in your local supermarket can differ from the information shown here.</i></p>
